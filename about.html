About

The Person Behind Solvr Labs

Full-stack developer and AI integration specialist. I build systems that let businesses automate the work they shouldn't be doing manually.

I'm a full-stack developer who specializes in AI integration for service businesses.

Most businesses know they should be using AI — but they don't have a tech team to make it happen. They're stuck doing everything manually: answering calls, writing proposals, chasing invoices, following up on leads. That's where I come in.

I take tools like n8n, Claude AI, and voice AI platforms, and I wire them into your existing business so they actually do useful work — not just generate text in a chat window. Missed call? The AI texts back and books the appointment. New lead? A proposal gets drafted automatically. Job complete? The invoice goes out and a review request follows.

When you work with Solvr Labs, you work directly with me. No account managers, no handoffs, no layers between you and the person building your system. That's not a limitation — it means faster communication, better results, and someone who actually understands your business.

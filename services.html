FAQ

Common Questions

What tools do you work with?+

I work with n8n, Make, and Zapier for workflow automation. For voice AI, I use Bland.ai and Vapi. For custom software, my stack is Next.js, Supabase, TypeScript, and Claude/OpenAI APIs. I pick the best tool for the job — not the most expensive one.

How long does an automation take to build?+

A single workflow automation (like a missed call text-back) can be live in 1-2 weeks. A voice AI receptionist typically takes 2-3 weeks. Full custom software varies by scope — I'll give you an honest timeline during the discovery call.

Do I need to change my existing systems?+

No. I integrate with what you already use — your CRM, calendar, invoicing tool, phone system. The whole point is to make your existing setup smarter, not replace it.

What does ongoing support look like?+

After launch, I offer monthly retainers that cover monitoring, bug fixes, and adding new automations as your business grows. Most clients add 1-2 new workflows per month once they see the first one working.

How is this different from just using ChatGPT?+

ChatGPT is a chat window. What I build are automated systems that run without you being involved — they trigger from real events (a missed call, a new lead, a completed job), do the thinking, and take the action. No one needs to type a prompt.

Industries We Serve

Automation built for your trade

Every industry has different workflows. Here's what we automate for each.

Emergency AC/Heat Calls

Customer calls at 11 PM about no AC. AI answers, flags as emergency, creates a priority job in ServiceTitan, and alerts the on-call tech via SMS.

Seasonal Maintenance Reminders

AI checks your customer list every spring and fall, sends personalized tune-up reminders via SMS, and books appointments when they respond.

Install Quote Automation

Customer fills out a form about their home size and system age. AI generates a detailed quote with equipment options and emails it within minutes.

Post-Job Follow-Up Sequence

After an install, AI sends a thank-you SMS, a review request 2 days later, and a maintenance plan offer at 30 days. All automatic.

Emergency Leak Triage

Customer calls about a burst pipe. AI asks the right questions, determines severity, creates a priority job, and dispatches your nearest available plumber.

Missed Call Recovery

Every missed call gets an instant AI text: "Sorry we missed you! Describe your plumbing issue and we'll get you booked." Captures leads you'd otherwise lose.

Auto Invoicing After Service

Tech marks job complete in Jobber. System generates the invoice, texts a payment link to the customer, and follows up if unpaid after 3 days.

Google Review Automation

Two days after service, AI sends a friendly review request with a direct Google link. One follow-up if no response. Consistent 5-star reviews on autopilot.

Storm Damage Lead Capture

After a storm, leads flood in. AI qualifies each one — address, damage type, insurance status — and books inspection appointments automatically.

Estimate Follow-Up Drip

Gave an estimate but haven't heard back? AI sends a check-in at 3 days, a value-add message at 7 days, and a final offer at 14 days. Closes more jobs.

Crew Scheduling & Dispatch

Job gets approved. System checks crew availability, assigns the team, sends schedule details to the crew lead, and confirms with the homeowner.

Insurance Claim Coordination

AI tracks where each job is in the insurance process and sends status updates to homeowners automatically. Fewer "where's my claim?" calls.

Service Call Triage

Customer describes their issue. AI determines if it's a panel upgrade, outlet repair, or emergency — routes to the right electrician with the right skills.

Permit & Inspection Tracking

Job requires a permit? AI tracks the status, reminds you to schedule inspections, and updates the customer on progress without you lifting a finger.

Commercial Bid Proposals

For commercial jobs, AI takes your scope notes and generates a professional proposal with line items, timelines, and terms. Ready to send in minutes.

After-Hours Emergency Routing

Power outage call at 2 AM. AI answers, confirms the emergency, and pages the on-call electrician with all the details. Customer never waits.

Tenant Maintenance Requests

Tenant calls or texts about a broken dishwasher. AI logs the request, categorizes it, creates a work order, and dispatches the right vendor automatically.

After-Hours Emergency Line

AI answers the after-hours line, determines if it's a real emergency (flood, gas leak) or can wait until morning, and escalates accordingly.

Rent Collection Reminders

Automated rent reminder sequence: friendly heads-up 3 days before due, due-date notification, and late notices at 3 and 7 days past due.

Lease Renewal Automation

90 days before a lease expires, AI sends a renewal offer. Tracks responses, sends follow-ups, and flags units that will become vacant for your team.

The Difference

What changes when you automate

A side-by-side look at the same workflow — manual vs. automated.

Manual Process

Lead comes in from your website. Sits in your inbox until you check it hours later.

You call them back, play phone tag, and try to figure out what they need from a vague form submission.

You manually type up a proposal in Word, attach it to an email, and send it. Takes 30–45 minutes.

You forget to follow up. The customer books with someone else who responded faster.

With AI Automation

Lead submits your form. AI reads it instantly, qualifies them, and sends a confirmation SMS within 10 seconds.

AI writes a detailed proposal based on their request, your pricing, and your service area. Emailed automatically.

Customer clicks "accept" and books a time slot. Job appears on your calendar with all details attached.

You check your phone and the job is already booked. Total time from lead to appointment: under 5 minutes.

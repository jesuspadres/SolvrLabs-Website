FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Can callers tell it's AI?+

Most can't. Jay uses a natural-sounding voice with realistic pacing, pauses, and conversational flow. We've had callers leave reviews saying "your receptionist was so helpful" without realizing it was AI. During your free consultation, you'll hear it yourself and judge.

What if the AI says something wrong?+

Jay is custom-trained on your business — your services, pricing, service area, and availability. It doesn't make things up. If a caller asks something outside its training, it takes a message and flags it for your review. You approve the AI's knowledge base before it goes live.

Does it work with my version of Jobber / ServiceTitan?+

Yes. We integrate with Jobber, ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Google Calendar, QuickBooks, and more. During setup, we connect directly to your accounts and configure the workflows to match how your business operates — not a generic template.

What happens after hours or on weekends?+

Jay answers 24/7/365 — nights, weekends, holidays. Emergency calls trigger instant SMS and Slack alerts to your on-call tech. Non-urgent calls get a job created and a confirmation sent to the customer. You wake up to a full schedule, not a pile of voicemails.

How long does setup take?+

Most businesses are live within 3-5 business days. We handle everything: AI training, software integration, phone routing, and testing. You just answer a few questions about your business and give us access to your tools.

Can I still answer calls myself?+

Absolutely. You can set Jay to answer only when you're unavailable — after hours, during jobs, or when your line is busy. Or let Jay handle everything and focus on the work. It's fully configurable.

What if I want to cancel?+

No long-term contracts. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. Your phone number routes back to you and we hand over any data from your account. No lock-in, no hassle.

What languages do you support?+

Over 30 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Hindi, Japanese, Italian, Dutch, Russian, and more. The AI can auto-detect the caller's language and switch mid-conversation. No separate setup or extra cost.

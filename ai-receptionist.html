FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Can callers tell it's AI?+

Most can't. Our AI uses a natural-sounding voice with realistic pacing, pauses, and conversational flow — powered by ElevenLabs, the industry leader in voice AI. You can even give it a custom name and clone a real voice. During your free consultation, you'll hear it yourself and judge.

What if the AI says something wrong?+

Our AI is custom-trained on your business — your services, pricing, service area, and availability. It doesn't make things up. If a caller asks something outside its training, it takes a message and flags it for your review. You approve the knowledge base before it goes live.

What software does it integrate with?+

We integrate with Google Calendar, Discord, Slack, ServiceTitan, Jobber, Housecall Pro, QuickBooks, SMS, email, and more. If your tool has an API, we can connect it. During setup, we configure the workflows to match how your business operates — not a generic template.

What happens after hours or on weekends?+

Our AI answers 24/7/365 — nights, weekends, holidays. Emergency calls trigger instant alerts to you via Discord, SMS, or webhook. Non-urgent calls get a job created and an appointment booked on your calendar. You wake up to a full schedule, not a pile of voicemails.

How long does setup take?+

Most businesses are live within 3-5 business days. We handle everything: AI training, software integration, phone routing, and testing. You just answer a few questions about your business and give us access to your tools.

Can I still answer calls myself?+

Absolutely. You can set our AI to answer only when you're unavailable — after hours, during jobs, or when your line is busy. Or let it handle everything so you can focus on the work. It's fully configurable.

What if I want to cancel?+

No long-term contracts. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. Your phone number routes back to you and we hand over any data from your account. No lock-in, no hassle.

What languages do you support?+

Over 30 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Hindi, Japanese, Italian, Dutch, Russian, and more. Our AI can auto-detect the caller's language and switch mid-conversation. No separate setup or extra cost.

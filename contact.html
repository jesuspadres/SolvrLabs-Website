FAQ

Before You Reach Out

What happens on the first call?+

I'll ask about your business, what takes up too much time, and what tools you already use. Then I'll tell you which workflows I can automate and give you a rough sense of timeline and cost. No sales pitch.

How much does it cost?+

It depends on scope. A simple workflow automation might be a one-time setup fee. Larger projects use a setup fee plus a monthly retainer for ongoing support and new automations. I give you a clear proposal before any work starts.

I'm not technical — is that a problem?+

Not at all. Most of my clients aren't technical. You tell me what's eating your time, I figure out the AI solution. I handle all the setup and you get a system that just works.

What if AI isn't the right fit for my problem?+

I'll tell you. Not every problem needs AI, and I'd rather be honest about that than sell you something that won't work. If there's a simpler solution, I'll point you in the right direction.
